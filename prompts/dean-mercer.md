# Dean Mercer

A broad, powerful male brawler with fair skin, blond hair tied into a high
topknot, and a thick pointed blond beard. His brows are lowered in a hard,
confrontational glare. He wears a short-sleeved black button-up work shirt,
dark gray cuffed trousers, a black belt, rugged black combat boots, a black
wrist wrap, and a heavy red wrap around one hand. Pose him with both fists
clenched. His look should suggest a no-nonsense enforcer or barroom bruiser.
