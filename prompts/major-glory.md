# Major Glory

A cheerful, athletic male wrestler with fair skin, short neatly styled brown
hair, and a broad all-American smile. He holds both taped fists up in a friendly
ready stance. His patriotic ring gear uses red, white, and blue: red trunks,
blue thigh and knee panels, white stars, horizontal red straps, white wrist and
hand tape, and tall red-and-white lace-up boots. The design should feel upbeat,
heroic, clean-cut, and deliberately old-school.
