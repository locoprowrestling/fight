# Running Spine Buster

- ID: `round03-running-spine-buster`
- Formation: running catch, defender facing attacker.
- Contacts: attacker catches torso/hips; defender arms flare on collision.
- Phases: approach/setup 0-33%, contact 33-67%, impact 67-100%.
- Markers: `contact` at catch; `impact` on back landing.
- Exit: defender face-up at attacker's feet.
- Interruption: before catch; after catch only special escapes may cancel.
- Root movement: rush or combat executor owns travel.
- Fallback: paired impact throw.
