# Top-Rope Powerbomb

- ID: `round03-top-rope-powerbomb`
- Formation: paired top-corner anchor.
- Contacts: stable waist and thigh grips throughout lift.
- Phases: setup 0-25%, contact 25-50%, lift 50-75%, impact 75-100%.
- Markers: `pose-sync` at perch, `contact`, `camera-heavy`, `impact`.
- Exit: defender face-up; attacker balanced or descending to mat.
- Interruption: setup only.
- Root movement: aerial/special executor owns launch and landing positions.
- Fallback: aerial attacker plus existing downed defender.
