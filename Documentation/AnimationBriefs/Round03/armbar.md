# Armbar

- ID: `round03-armbar`
- Formation: attacker near grounded defender's head/shoulder.
- Contacts: attacker knees clamp upper arm; hands control wrist.
- Phases: apply 0-50%, hold loop 50-100%.
- Markers: `contact` at wrist capture; `pose-sync` entering loop; `release`.
- Exit: submission hold until escape, rope break, or tap.
- Interruption: application only; hold exits through submission cleanup.
- Loops: attacker pressure and defender struggle authored separately.
- Fallback: paired hold poses.
