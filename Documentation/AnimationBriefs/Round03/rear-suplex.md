# Rear Suplex

- ID: `round03-rear-suplex`
- Formation: rear standing, roots 0.85 m apart, both facing forward.
- Contacts: attacker waist lock; defender hands fight the lock.
- Phases: setup 0-33%, contact 33-67%, impact 67-100%.
- Markers: `pose-sync` at waist lock; `impact` at shoulder/back landing.
- Exit: defender face-up; attacker bridged or seated.
- Interruption: before lift commitment.
- Mirroring: allowed.
- Fallback: paired snap throw / face-up sell.
