# DDT

- ID: `round03-ddt`
- Formation: front standing, roots 0.85 m apart, facing each other.
- Contacts: attacker front facelock; defender near hand braces at attacker hip.
- Phases: setup 0-33%, contact 33-67%, impact 67-100%.
- Markers: `contact` when facelock closes; `impact` when defender reaches mat.
- Exit: defender face-down; attacker kneeling or low seated.
- Interruption: setup/contact only.
- Mirroring: allowed if both clips and grip hands are mirrored together.
- Fallback: paired snap throw / impact sell.
